Privacy Policy

Bookmark Manager Extension

Bookmark Manager respects your privacy. This extension does not collect personally identifiable information.

Permissions

  • Bookmarks: Used only to display, organize, search, and edit bookmarks locally in your browser.
  • Storage: Used to save user preferences (such as theme and font) locally.
  • Identity (if enabled in the future): Used only for authenticating with Firebase to sync bookmarks across devices. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Usage

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. All bookmark data remains on your device, unless you explicitly enable sync via Firebase (in future versions).
